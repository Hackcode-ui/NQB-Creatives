About NQB CREATIVES

\

NQB CREATIVES is a fast-growing business in Ghana that deals in all kinds of graphic designs, printing works, video editing, transcription and many more. This business was founded by Mr. Douglas Peters.

\

Located in Kumasi at Santasi Anyinam off Obuasi road, we are committed to delivering exceptional creative services to our clients. Our team consists of dedicated employees who are keen on the attainment of the business success.

\

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, creative solutions, and commitment to client satisfaction. Whether you need graphic design, printing services, video editing, or transcription work, our team is ready to bring your vision to life.
